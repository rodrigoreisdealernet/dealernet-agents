# Marketing Plan - 2026-06-14

Today's story is faster, more connected branch operations. Teams can now move equipment, run the yard, inspect assets, and monitor project spend with less manual follow-up and fewer context breaks.

## Move equipment between projects and branches
- **Promotes:** Move equipment between projects and branches - Shipped in #1549
- **Audience / persona:** Admins and branch managers coordinating equipment moves across branches and projects without losing approval or transit visibility
- **Value proposition:** Keep every transfer in one workflow so teams can move equipment with clearer ownership, status, and history.
- **Talking points:**
  - Track a move from request to receipt without stitching together calls, notes, and status checks.
  - See what is currently in transit so planners know where equipment stands before making the next commitment.
  - Review transfer history in one place when questions come up about timing, approvals, or handoff.
- **Suggested channels:** in-app announcement, customer email, LinkedIn/social, changelog line

### Ready-to-use copy
- **In-app announcement** (<160 chars): "Track equipment transfers from request to receipt in one place, with live transit status and full history for every move."
- **Email blurb** (subject + 2-3 sentences): "Subject: New transfer management for branch and project moves. You can now manage equipment transfers from request through approval, dispatch, and receipt in one workflow. Teams get clearer in-transit visibility and a complete transfer history when they need to confirm where an asset is and what happened next."
- **Social / LinkedIn post** (<1-2 short paragraphs + CTA): "Equipment moves are easier to manage when the whole handoff lives in one workflow. Transfer Management helps teams track requests, approvals, dispatch, receipt, and transfer history without piecing updates together manually.\n\nIf your branches or projects move equipment often, this update gives operators a clearer view of what is in transit and what has already landed. Learn more in the release notes docs link."
- **Changelog line** (one sentence): "Transfer Management now tracks equipment moves from request through receipt, with in-transit visibility and full transfer history."

- **Call to action:** Open Transfer Management and run your next inter-branch or project move through the full workflow.
