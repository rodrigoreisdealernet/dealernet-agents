# Release Notes — June 2026

## 2026-06-20

### Document repairs without leaving the work order
- **What's new:** Service teams now get a repair documentation copilot inside the work-order detail page. It pulls forward recent faults and service history, then guides the technician through documenting the repair, updating status, or escalating without bouncing across separate screens.
- **Who it's for:** branch_manager, field_operator
- **Learn more (Docs):** #2423 (guide in progress)
- **Shipped in:** #2232

### Hold rent-ready approval until inspection evidence is complete
- **What's new:** Rent-ready review now opens as one inspection evidence bundle with checklist results, photos, deficiencies, and reviewer notes in the same place. The screen blocks rent-ready approval until required inspection sign-off and deficiency follow-up are complete, so teams can reopen or re-review units without losing context.
- **Who it's for:** branch_manager, field_operator
- **Learn more (Docs):** #1971 (guide in progress)
- **Shipped in:** #2233

### Run safety and compliance follow-up from dedicated workspaces
- **What's new:** Safety teams now have dedicated screens for incident deadlines, driver and operator readiness, and leadership KPI review. You can work OSHA and post-accident obligations, spot blocking certification or HOS gaps, and review draft focus areas from one set of operator surfaces instead of piecing the work together manually.
- **Who it's for:** admin, branch_manager
- **Learn more (Docs):** #2422 (guide in progress)
- **Shipped in:** #2288, #2289, #2290

### Keep technician queue context when you drill into a finding
- **What's new:** Opening a morning-queue finding and coming back now keeps the active priority and status filters with the handoff. Technicians can resume the queue from the same context after a detail review or reload instead of rebuilding the list by hand.
- **Who it's for:** branch_manager, field_operator
- **Learn more (Docs):** #2253 (guide in progress)
- **Shipped in:** #2291

### Preview admin changes before they ripple across the business
- **What's new:** Admins can now preview who, what scope, pricing, and reporting audiences a configuration change may affect before applying it. The preview keeps uncertainty visible and stays assist-only, so teams can check blast radius without making the change first.
- **Who it's for:** admin
- **Learn more (Docs):** #1906 (guide in progress)
- **Shipped in:** #2308

### See site contacts in the driver run sheet before departure
- **What's new:** Driver dispatch stop cards now show the site contact and phone number when it is available. The readiness banner also flags stops that are missing a contact, so drivers can catch the gap before leaving the yard.
- **Who it's for:** field_operator
- **Learn more (Docs):** #1028 (guide in progress)
- **Shipped in:** #2309

### Reopen inventory kit edits without losing your place
- **What's new:** Inventory Kits now keeps the selected kit in the page URL, so a reload or reopened tab can return you to the same draft instead of dropping you back at the list. If the saved draft no longer exists, the screen tells you instead of silently failing.
- **Who it's for:** admin, branch_manager
- **Learn more (Docs):** #1416 (guide in progress)
- **Shipped in:** #2360

### Turn approved fleet transfer proposals into live transfer records
- **What's new:** Approved fleet transfer proposals can now write through to the live transfer plan instead of stopping at a recommendation. Rejected or timed-out proposals remain audit-only, so teams keep a clear decision trail without accidental dispatch changes.
- **Who it's for:** admin, branch_manager
- **Learn more (Docs):** #1577 (guide in progress)
- **Shipped in:** #2399

The KPI cards link straight to the affected routes or contract lines, so you can move from summary to action without rebuilding the problem by hand.
- **Who it's for:** admin, branch_manager, read_only

- **Shipped in:** #2151, #2228

### Prioritize outreach with the account health queue

- **Learn more (Docs):** #2252 (guide in progress)
- **Shipped in:** #2167

### Review counter blockers in one place

### Scan into quick checkout on field mobile
