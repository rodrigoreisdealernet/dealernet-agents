Why this matters

The bet: the factory is the asset, the product is the proof

For the portfolio
  • Replicable. The same factory that built an automotive DMS retargets to any Volaris vertical-market domain — the agents and pipeline are domain-agnostic.
  • Margin. Design, build, test, review and back-office ops run as software, not headcount.
  • Speed. An idea becomes a reviewed, tested PR in hours, not sprints.
Governed, not unmanaged
  • Every decision is recorded. 110 ADRs capture the architecture the agents work within.
  • Nothing ships unreviewed. Two human gates plus automated test & code-review agents.
  • Auditable. Issues, specs, PRs and labels form a complete paper trail.

A Volaris VMS playbook: own the domain, let the factory compound.

The software factory

From issue to merge — six stages, two human gates

StageWhoWhat happens
1 · Triage & shapeProduct Owner · Factory ArchitectDedupe, classify, route; write a spec / ADR when design is needed.
2 · SpecSpec agentTestable acceptance criteria written in customer language.
✋ Gate #1HumanApprove the spec before any code is written.
3 · BuildCoding agentImplements the change and opens a draft PR.
4 · TestsTester agentUnit / integration / E2E for the new behavior.
5 · ReviewTest reviewer + code reviewerAudit test quality, then a terminal code-review verdict on the PR.
✋ Gate #2HumanMerge — green checks, approved, mergeable.

The second factory

The Operations Factory — AI that runs the back office

16 domain AI agents orchestrated by 20 Temporal workflows automate tedious dealership work — and surface decisions to a human, never around one.

What the agents watch
  • Vehicle aging & disposition recommendations
  • Service — estimate rescue, shop & technician queues
  • Parts — inventory advisor
  • Finance — credit, collections, rev-rec, account health, lien deadlines
  • Branch / territory morning briefs & integration exceptions
Human-in-the-loop
  • Agents produce findings, not silent actions
  • The Morning Brief is the owner's triage inbox
  • Approvals flow back via durable Temporal signals
